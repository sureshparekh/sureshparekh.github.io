About Us

Introduction

Suresh Parekh, a Second Year BSc. Physics Student of St. Xavier's College Ahmedabad.

He is interested in integrating Physics with technology and make physics learning more easier.
The present computational exercise is designed for students to understand the physics of projectile motion. The objective of this computational exercise is to make physics learning more interesting and interactive. In the present work, Javascript along with HTML is used to understand the projectile motion for the different cases.






There are a number of benefits of introducing technology in the field of education. There has been a positive impact of technology on education. Virtual classrooms have made learning easier for the student community. With the potential use of technology, the learning speed and style have undergone a sea change and communication has become easier. Thus, this kind of simulations become essential in this era for educating students in an interactive manner in which they can perform different cases on their own and can learn understand a physical phenomena as easily as a small child learns to throw a ball by himself/herself.
